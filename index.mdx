## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your deployment model">
    Review the [deployment models](/deployment-models) and pick the scenario that matches your infrastructure. Each scenario has its own directory under `scenarios/`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your environment">
    Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and fill in your cluster credentials and infrastructure references. For Terraform scenarios, also copy `terraform.tfvars.example`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy the platform">
    Run Terraform, Helm, or Ansible depending on your scenario. See the [quickstart guide](/quickstart) for the exact commands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard your first topic">
    Follow the [topic onboarding guide](/governance/topic-onboarding) to declare your first governed topic with schema, RBAC, and DR mirror ‚Äî in under a day.
  </Step>
</Steps>
